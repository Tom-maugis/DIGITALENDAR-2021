Double Sundae functionnalities








1. You can check the exact actual date with Double Sundae, feel free to try it ! The current day is marked with a slight blue highlighter .

2. Add/Remove an event, you are able to indefinitely creating new events on whatever existing day you would like to .

3. Past check, you can see what previous event you had if you left them after the month passed, for example you want to check who's birthday it was 1 month ago you just have to click the "Back" button to see who's birthday it was !

That's it for the functionnalities! Feel free to make suggestions using the "Contact" page just at the top right of this page .
